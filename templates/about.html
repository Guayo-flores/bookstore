Our story

What started as a university project turned into a practical bookstore platform with clean code, strong SQL relationships, and a crisp UI. We keep it simple, fast, and educational.

Every feature we ship aims to teach something new—database design, authentication, role-based admin, image uploads, and more.

Spring 2025 · First commit

Bootstrapped Flask app, wired PostgreSQL, and built the first schema for books, authors, and categories.

Summer 2025 · Admin & uploads

Added role-based admin, CSRF protection, image/file uploads, and a clean Bootstrap admin.

Fall 2025 · Public store

Launched a fast storefront with search, filters, sorting, and a dedicated book detail page.

Next · Wishlists & reviews

Planning user accounts with wishlists, ratings, and lightweight reviews.

What we value
